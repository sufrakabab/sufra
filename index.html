Welcome to Sufra Kabab

Savor Tradition, Enjoy Halal

At Sufra Kabab, we bring you the authentic flavors of Indian and Bihari cuisine, crafted with love and tradition. Our specialty? Mouthwatering kebabs, rich curries, fragrant biryanis, and a variety of homemade delicacies that capture the essence of true home-cooked food. Our journey began with a simple idea—to serve authentic, homemade flavors that remind you of the food made in your own kitchen. Every dish is prepared by passionate home chefs, who have perfected their craft over generations, ensuring that every bite delivers the real, traditional taste you crave.

Why Choose Us?

  • Authentic Home-Cooked Taste – Time-honored recipes, just like homemade.
  • Premium Quality Ingredients – Finest spices, fresh ingredients, and 100% halal meat.
  • Traditional Bihari & Indian Kebabs – Juicy, flavorful kebabs made using age-old techniques.
  • Catering for All Occasions – Perfect for family gatherings, corporate events, and celebrations.
  • Hygiene & Quality Assurance – Every dish is made with care and the highest standards.
  • 100% Halal Meat – Only premium, hand-selected halal meats for superior taste.
  • Made with Love – Cooked by expert home chefs for a genuine, comforting experience.

At Sufra Kabab, food isn’t just a meal—it’s an experience. Whether you're craving the smoky richness of Seekh Kebabs or the warmth of home-cooked curries, we bring it all to you, fresh and full of flavor.
