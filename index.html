Welcome to Sufra Kabab

Savor Tradition, Enjoy Halal

Savor authentic Indian halal cuisine with our signature Sufra Kabab and a variety of mouthwatering kebabs, grilled to perfection!

At Sufra Kabab, food is more than just a meal—it’s a blessing, a tradition, and a way to bring hearts closer. Rooted in the age-old practice of eating together and sharing food over a Sufra or a dastarkhwan, our catering service is meant to create moments of connection, warmth, and celebration through our food.

At Sufra Kabab, we are dedicated to bringing you authentic Indian halal cuisine, crafted with passion and tradition. We use time-honored recipes and the finest ingredients to create mouthwatering kebabs and flavorful dishes that transport your taste buds with every bite. From special events and family gatherings to a simple craving for home-cooked goodness, our catering services deliver the rich, comforting flavors of Indian cuisine straight to your table. Experience the warmth of tradition and the joy of exceptional food with sufra kabab.
